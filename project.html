Biryani

Biryani is a mixed rice dish originating among the Muslims of the Indian subcontinent. It is made with Indian spices, rice, and usually some type of meat (chicken, beef, goat, lamb, prawn, fish) or in some cases without any meat, and sometimes, in addition, eggs and potatoes.

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken, traditionally known as murgh makhani, is an Indian dish. It is a type of curry made from chicken with a spiced tomato and butter (makhan) sauce. Its sauce is known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala , which uses a tomato paste.

Masala Dosai

Masala Dosa / dosey / dosai is a variation of the popular South Indian Cuisine dosa which is a hotel recipe and has its origins in Udupi cuisine of Karnataka. It is made from rice, lentils, Urad dal, Chana dal, fenugreek and served with potato Curry, chutneys, sambar
